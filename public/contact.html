FAQ

HOW DO I SIGN UP?

All you have to do is hit "login" in the top right. It will take you to a login page, where you can sign in with your gmail account. From there, a "profile" page option will appear. Navigate there, fill out your profile, and enjoy!

WHAT FUNCTIONS DOES THE SITE HAVE?

Stutor is equipped with a variety of functions, including: gmail login, ticket creation and management, database interaction, and much more. Get started and explore!

WHO MADE THIS ALL? WHY?

All relevant information about us and the vision for the project can be found on the about us page.

HOW WAS THIS SITE BUILT?

Stutor is programmed mainly in HTML, CSS and Javascript, with an Angular Javascript framework. It also uses multiple Bootstrap elements, and utilizes a Firebase database and API to store, retrieve and manipulate data.

IS STUTOR OPEN TO ALL SCHOOLS?
